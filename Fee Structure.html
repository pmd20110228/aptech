PRIMARY

The tuition fees for june and the first term fees must be paed before the end of May. This applies even to those who are seeking financial assistance from the school. Those in receipt of Goverment Concessions are promoted to the next higher class need not pay fees.

Fee will be paid thorough the South Indian Bank. Fee bills will be availble online which parents can print form the website and pay to bank by cash.

Where possile, parrents are requested to pay fees in advance for six months or a year. If the fees for the month are not paid before 10th the month, the boy is liable to be sent home. If fees cannot be paid in time the Principal's or Vice Principal's permission must be obtained in writing. Please state in your application when you will be able ti pay the fees. If the fees, are paid wwithing 2 months the name will be struck off the rolls
